About the Project & Authors

This website was created as part of the Week 4 Practical: Git Collaboration. Each page was developed by a different team member, highlighting how computing contributes positively to society in healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability. Every author’s short bio is written by another team member, to encourage collaboration and perspective sharing.
